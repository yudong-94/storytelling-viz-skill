Kilauea Eruption Cadence

The longest recent Kilauea episodes arrived at the start of the sequence

The pattern is less a steady burn than a shift in rhythm. Early bursts lasted far longer, while later 2025 episodes became shorter and more frequent, turning the timeline into a story about changing cadence rather than constant intensity.

Takeaway: The longest episodes cluster near the beginning of the sequence, while most later eruptions tightened into shorter bursts that often lasted well under a day.

Scope: This chart tracks episode start times and durations from late December 2024 onward. It describes timing, not lava volume, ash output, or eruption severity.
