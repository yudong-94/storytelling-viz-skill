U.S. Visitors in Japan

Tokyo led U.S. overnight stays across Japan, but the order below it shifted

Among the six prefectures that ranked highest in 2024, Tokyo stayed first throughout 2011-2024 while Kyoto, Osaka, Kanagawa, Chiba, and Okinawa traded places below it.

Takeaway: Tokyo remained the clear leader for U.S. visitor overnight stays, while Kyoto, Osaka, Kanagawa, Chiba, and Okinawa shifted positions below it.

Scope: This chart follows only the six prefectures that ranked highest in 2024 for U.S. visitor overnight stays. The lines show rank within that selected six-prefecture cohort, not rank across all 47 prefectures.

Source: Japan Tourism Statistics, JNTO, “Overnight Stays by Prefecture and Country/Area”.
