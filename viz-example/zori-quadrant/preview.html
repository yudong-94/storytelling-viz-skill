U.S. Rent Trends

Which rental markets cooled after the pandemic surge?

Large Sun Belt metros surged fastest through mid-2022, but several later lost momentum while many coastal and Midwest markets kept rising. The split is less about one national rent cycle than about different local paths after the same boom.

Takeaway: Austin and several other fast-rising Sun Belt markets cooled after their early spike, while cities such as New York, Boston, and Chicago continued climbing after 2022.

Scope: This view uses the top 25 metros by size rank plus Austin, and compares rent growth in two phases: December 2019 to August 2022, then August 2022 to February 2026.

Source: Zillow Observed Rent Index (ZORI).
